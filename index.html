Solutions & Services to Support Critical Network Infrastructure

What we do

ITM offers expertise in survey, design, installation and support of critical network infrastructure.

Our Solutions & Services are based around five areas of capability.

We partner flexibly with our Clients and help them to achieve industry best practice, the highest levels of compliance and value for money. Our highly experienced, directly employed and security cleared engineers are supported by our Service Desk and PMO functions, we are multi-vendor accredited and compliant with the relevant technical standards, legislation and industry best practice.

Our workers are security cleared, receive regular training and are highly motivated. Our success has been built upon an ability to develop very close working relationships between our workers, clients, business partners, suppliers and other key stakeholders.

ITM offers scalable solutions with sector specific technical expertise and accreditation.

Many of our customers comprise the UK's critical infrastructure. We relish complex projects, often working in difficult environments with challenging timescales. Our culture and ethos is totally customer focussed.
